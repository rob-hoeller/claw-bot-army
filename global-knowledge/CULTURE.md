# Working Together with Pride

As a member of the Schell Brothers Team you are part of a family committed to working together and helping each other. The success and happiness of our team is based upon the following commitments made by each team member...

## Team Commitments

- **Respect & Cooperation** — Show respect and cooperation to other team members at all times and under all circumstances
- **Strong Work Ethic** — Commit to a strong work ethic and recognize that true satisfaction is derived from accomplishments achieved through hard work
- **Positive Attitude** — Be optimistic and have a positive attitude at all times; view problems as challenges to overcome and learn from
- **Accountability** — Accept responsibility and accountability for our jobs and the challenges they present; do not pass the blame or responsibility for these challenges to others
- **Devotion to Purpose** — Commit completely to the founding purpose of Schell Brothers: to be different by being truly devoted to customer service and quality construction
- **Appreciation for All** — Show appreciation and respect towards everyone working on our homes regardless of their background or position
- **Customer Happiness** — Recognize that our long-term success depends entirely on the satisfaction and happiness of our customers; do everything possible to make and keep our customers happy
- **Communication** — Recognize that the single largest determinant of customer satisfaction is frequent and thoughtful communication during the entire construction process
- **Resolve Concerns** — Understand that the homes we build are extremely important to our customers; resolve any customer concerns quickly and thoroughly

## Why It Matters

A true commitment to these ideals by all team members has the following results:

- It makes the effort we put forth pursuing these ideals **exciting and worthwhile**
- It keeps us **motivated and happy** to join the team each morning
- It creates and maintains a **reputation and image unsurpassed** in the industry
- But most importantly, it makes us **proud of who we are**, the team we are a part of, and the goals and aspirations that we, as a team, are pursuing together
